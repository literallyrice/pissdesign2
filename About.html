Facts & Questions

Here is where you will find help for all of your issues :)

Why is everything so expensive?

Unfortunately, I do not control the prices of the products, redbubble does. If i ever start making my own shirts and stuff i could bypass that.

Is everything high quality?

Of course, I cannot speak for every product. I have had friends and family that purchase things, and everything is very high quality. All the tshirts feel very nice, and the stickers last a long time.

Do you make your own designs?

Yes! I make all my own designs using photoshop. You can suggest an idea to me for a design and I can make it and put it on my site. Use parker@piss.design for this. I will try to give you credit in the description.
